She had been there four times in the past year with Owen. She knew the layout the way you know a place you've eaten in without quite studying it.

She'd studied it on Thursday.

Cole had the wire equipment by Friday. A contact of a contact—he wouldn't tell her the provenance—small enough to sit flush against her sternum under the blouse she'd chosen for the specific purpose of having structure without bulk. He'd tested it in his car with a handheld receiver and the audio was clear at twelve feet.
