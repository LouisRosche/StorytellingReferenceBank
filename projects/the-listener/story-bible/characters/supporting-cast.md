## Detective Marcus Cole

### Basic Information

**Name**: Marcus Cole
**Role**: Supporting (B-Story ally)
**Age**: 63
**Gender**: Male
**Occupation**: Retired homicide detective, now private obsessive

### Physical Description

**Appearance**: 5'10", heavyset, ex-athlete gone soft. Black, close-cropped gray hair, face lined deep. Brown eyes that miss nothing. Hands that shake slightly (caffeine, age, or both). Dresses like he's still on the job: cheap suits, comfortable shoes.

**Voice**: Bass, tired gravel. Speaks in fragments. Questions are accusations. Cigarette-damaged.

### Psychology

**External Goal**: Solve Maya's case. Put Thomas Vane away.

**Internal Need**: Absolution. He had Thomas in the interview room and let him walk.

**Core Wound**: Failed the victim. Knew in his gut Thomas was guilty. Couldn't prove it. Watched him walk.

**Lie He Believes**: "The ends justify the means. Get the bad guy, nothing else matters."

**Arc**: From obsessed vigilante to accepting closure through legitimate means. Doesn't become a saint - just steps back from the edge.

### Voice Pattern

**Vocabulary**: Cop-speak, weary, economical. Full sentences rare.

**Sample dialogue**:
```
[COLE]: "Vane. Architect. Blue eyes, dead inside. You know him."
[COLE]: "Law won't touch him. I know things the law doesn't care about."
[COLE]: "You want justice? Or you want to feel better? Not the same thing."
```

## Owen Mercer

### Basic Information

**Name**: Owen Mercer
**Role**: Supporting (B-Story emotional anchor)
**Age**: 40
**Gender**: Male
**Occupation**: High school English teacher

### Physical Description

**Appearance**: 6'0", broad shoulders, runner's build maintained. Brown hair, warm brown eyes, easy smile that reaches his eyes. Dresses teacher-casual: khakis, button-downs, reading glasses perpetually lost.

**Voice**: Tenor, animated. Uses hands when talking. Laughs easily. Volume higher than necessary - enthusiasm leaks.

### Psychology

**External Goal**: Protect his family. Support his wife.

**Internal Need**: To be let in. To have a real partnership, not a performed one.

**Core Wound**: Knows Sarah keeps him at arm's length. Doesn't know why. Fears he's inadequate.

**Lie He Believes**: "If I'm supportive enough, patient enough, she'll eventually open up."

**Arc**: From patient doormat to demanding partner. Confronts Sarah. Chooses to stay anyway - but now with eyes open.

### Voice Pattern

**Vocabulary**: Literate, warm, quotes books often. Uses pet names.

**Sample dialogue**:
```
[OWEN]: "Hey, you. Long day? Emma's already down. Saved you some pasta."
[OWEN]: "Sarah, talk to me. Something's wrong. I can see it."
[OWEN]: "Ten years I've been waiting for you to trust me. I'm still here. That should tell you something."
```

## Dr. Ruth Henley

### Basic Information

**Name**: Dr. Ruth Henley
**Role**: Supporting (mentor figure)
**Age**: 63
**Gender**: Female
**Occupation**: Semi-retired clinical psychologist, supervisor

### Physical Description

**Appearance**: 5'4", compact, silver hair in practical cut. Sharp eyes behind fashionable glasses. Dresses elegantly but simply. Moves slower now - arthritis. Mind remains rapid.

**Voice**: Contralto, precise. British accent softened by decades in America. Dry wit deployed strategically.

### Psychology

**External Goal**: Guide Sarah through crisis. Protect her from professional consequences.

**Internal Need**: Not to fail another student. (Lost a supervisee to suicide years ago.)

**Lie She Believes**: "Detachment is protection." (Ironic, given her profession.)

**Arc**: Minor. Forces Sarah to confront what she's avoiding. Represents the profession's conscience.

### Voice Pattern

**Vocabulary**: Academic precision, occasional British idiom. Never wastes words.

**Sample dialogue**:
```
[RUTH]: "You're deflecting, dear. I've taught you better than that."
[RUTH]: "The wound we don't examine is the one that festers. Remind me who said that?"
[RUTH]: "You can lie to patients. You cannot lie to supervisors. It's in the handbook."
```

## Emma Mercer

### Basic Information

**Name**: Emma Mercer
**Role**: Minor (stakes embodiment)
**Age**: 15
**Gender**: Female
**Occupation**: Tenth grader

### Physical Description

**Appearance**: Sarah's chestnut hair but Owen's brown eyes and easy openness. Taller than Sarah remembers her being. Usually in jeans and hoodies. Glasses for reading, often lost.

**Voice**: Owen's animated quality but Sarah's precision creeping in. Fast when excited, deliberately flat when being a teenager about something.

### Psychology

Emma is not a viewpoint character. She represents what Sarah stands to lose. At 15 she's old enough to notice things and ask direct questions — old enough that Sarah can no longer deflect with simple comfort. Emma knows about Maya in outline. She asks about what she wasn't told.

### Voice Pattern

**Sample dialogue**:
```
[EMMA]: "Mom, why do you keep looking out the window?"
[EMMA]: "We never talk about it. Doesn't that drive you crazy?"
[EMMA]: "I'm sorry, Mom. I didn't know it was still like that."
```

## Maya Aldridge (Deceased)

### Basic Information

**Name**: Maya Aldridge
**Role**: Supporting (via memory/recordings/flashback)
**Age**: 27 at death (would be 37)
**Gender**: Female
**Occupation**: (Was) Graduate student, art history

### Physical Description

**Appearance**: 5'5", petite, artistic. Wild dark curly hair, Sarah's gray-green eyes but brighter. Dressed in vintage finds, bold colors. Always a new interesting earring.

**Voice**: In recordings/flashbacks: higher than Sarah's, faster. Emotional, dramatic, interrupted herself often. Laughed frequently.

### Psychology

Maya was the "emotional" sister to Sarah's "responsible" one. Creative, chaotic, warm, exhausting. Her fear of Thomas was dismissed as drama until it was too late. In death, she's been simplified - Sarah barely remembers Maya's flaws anymore.

### Voice Pattern

**Sample dialogue** (from recordings/flashbacks):
```
[MAYA]: "Sarah, I'm not being crazy. He was outside again. I saw him."
[MAYA]: "Never mind. You're busy. I'll figure it out. I always do, right?"
[MAYA]: "I love you, big sister. Even when you're being a robot about it."
```
