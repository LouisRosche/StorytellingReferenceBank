# Scene Card Template

One card per scene. Referenced in `@templates/revision-workflow.md` (Pass 2 tool).

Use these to plan before drafting, or as diagnostic during revision. A scene that can't fill this card probably shouldn't exist.

## Purpose

### Scene Goal

What does the POV character want from this scene?

*(Be specific and concrete. "She wants to learn the truth" is vague. "She wants Marcos to admit he was there" is a goal.)*

### Scene Conflict

What stands in the way?

- **External**: *(Person, situation, obstacle)*
- **Internal**: *(Competing desire, fear, belief holding them back)*

### Outcome

**Result**: [ ] Yes  [ ] No  [ ] Yes-but  [ ] No-and

*(The POV character gets what they want / doesn't / gets it but at cost / fails and things worsen)*

**What changed?**
*(Something must shift — relationship, information, power, character understanding. If nothing changed, cut the scene.)*

## Quick Diagnostic

Before moving on, confirm:

- [ ] The POV character has a clear, specific goal
- [ ] Something is in the way
- [ ] The outcome is not neutral (something changed)
- [ ] The scene enters late and exits early
- [ ] At least two dramatic functions are served
- [ ] There's a key image or moment to anchor the scene
- [ ] Nothing is explained that could be shown
